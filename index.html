This Bike Path/Bikes Only Sign was inspired by the intervention of an anonymous group on twitter, the so-called Department of Transformation @NYC_DOTr. Under the guise of tactical urbanism, which advocates low-cost interventions to benefit cyclists and pedestrians, the group placed an unofficial sign under the Queensboro bridge to discourage vehicles, official and otherwise, from blocking the bike path.
